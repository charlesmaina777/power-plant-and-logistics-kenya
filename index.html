Power Plant & Logistics KE

Power Plant & Logistics Kenya is a leading provider of clean energy fuels, specializing in offering LPG solutions for standby power generators. The company is committed to providing environmentally acceptable fuels as an alternative to diesel-run generators. The mission is to convert up to 5000 telcos base stations to LPG run generators. Committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility, Power Plant and Logistics Kenya aims to reduce carbon emissions and promote cleaner energy solutions in the telecommunication industry and beyond. The goal of significantly reducing environmental impact while ensuring reliable and efficient power generation.

About Us

BUSINESS OBJECTIVE

The primary objective of Power Plant & Logistics Kenya is to provide cleaner, more sustainable fuel solutions to the telecommunication industry while reducing its carbon emissions footprint. This involves offering LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) solutions for standby power generators, converting telcos base stations to LPG run generators, and promoting environmentally acceptable fuels as an alternative to diesel-run generators

OUR VISION

Our vision is to be the center of clean energy production in Kenya. We aim to provide sustainable and reliable energy solutions that elevate the lives of communities while setting a standard for environmental responsibility and leadership in the industry.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Power Plant & Logistics Kenya is to convert up to 5000 telcos base stations to LPG run generators. We are committed to providing reliable, environmentally friendly fuel solutions that contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable future for the telecommunication industry and beyond.
